- Weak points of the paper: 

In the context of WODPEC the paper gives too much 
attention to the overall IS migration process and not 
enough to how ODP concepts are applied in that process.


- Strong points of the paper: 

The concept of merging the OMG Action Semantics and 
RM-ODP metamodels to provide for a fuller specification 
of actions in an ODP specification.


- Any suggestions for improvements: 

In the context of WODPEC the paper would be 
improved by cutting down the discussion of the 
migration process and expanding the discussion 
of the metamodel merger and its application in 
the migration process.
