A spirited statement of the ODP manifesto, giving a justification
for the creation of the RM-ODP in a very positive style.
Although this is mostly familiar material, it is good to see it
articulated clearly.


- Weak points of the paper:


The structure is a little weak, in that the same points are repeated
at intervals, and several of the examples are referenced two or three
times, reducing their freshness and thus their impact. However, it
is a well written piece.


It is identifying the need for the RM-ODP in quite broad terms, and
so is good scene setting, but makes no specific recommendations
for progressing the standard; we must assume then that it is currently
perfect?


- Strong points of the paper:
Clarity, style and passion.


- Any suggestions for improvements:

"method calls which"? However, it's a quote, so should say whatever
Stu said.
