This paper is a personal view on what architecture should be. 
It gives a literature survey on the subject and highlights H. Kilov's 
interpretation of Business and System Specification and Design. 
ODP is quoted but nothing is said on how to rely on ODP framework 
to build an architecture. 

- Weak points of the paper: 

* The use of RM-ODP, the viewpoints, the concepts, is not sufficiently 
underlined in chapter 3 

* RM-ODP is not considered in this paper as a framework 

- Strong points of the paper: 

* An interesting report on architecture with a solid list of references 

* This paper can lead to lively discussions on "what is architecture" 
and how to benefit from RM-ODP 

- Any suggestions for improvements: 

* The use of RM-ODP could be clearly pointed out 

* Suggestions on what is missing in RM-ODP to specify an architecture 
would be good help for future standard evolutions 

* Misspellings should be corrected
