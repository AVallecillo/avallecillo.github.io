4) JUSTIFICATION AND GENERAL COMMENTS:
  (A few lines are mandatory here to justify 
   the numerical evaluation. You may also include 
   general comments, relevant for both author(s)

- Weak points of the paper:

  The use of RM-ODP in the paper can be seen as marginal. 
  The paper is much more around migration processes and RM-ODP 
  is used to structure an intermediary meta-model that can be
  used for multiple migrations.  

- Strong points of the paper:
  The main strong point of the paper is its originality. Usually RM-ODP is much more seen as
  a way to represent the architecture of a systems, not as a metamodel support.

- Any suggestions for improvements: 
  Perhaps emphasis the use of RM-ODP for the other metamodels (mainly the Target one) and
  perhaps also the reuse of the target metamodel as a source for the architecture
  representation of the resulting system.
