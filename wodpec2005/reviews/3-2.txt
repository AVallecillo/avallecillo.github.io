- Summary and main contributions:

The paper addresses the issue of inter-viewpoint 
correspondences, illustrating the
need and proposing a viewpoint correspondence view.


- Weak points of the paper:

English is quite bad in places, but I have read worse, 
it could do with being read and edited by a native speaker.
The references should not be in a table !

- Strong points of the paper:

An issue that I definitely believe needs addressing, this is a good starting point.


- Any suggestions for improvements:

With respect to Implicit links and the example of Enterprise to Engineering as an implicit link,
I believe there could well be specific correspondences between 
Enterprise and Engineering that
do not involve the Computational specification. Thus it would be necessary not redundant. E.g. various policies could be 
specifically addressed to concepts specified in the engineering 
view.


Although you mention MDA, have you considered the use of 
Transformations as a means to specify inter-viewpoint correspondences?
