- Summary and main contributions:

The paper proposes a UML Profile for RM-ODP Comp-VP and 
illustrates its use on the Templeman Library Example.

- Weak points of the paper:

The Templeman Library Example is rather over used in the 
RM-ODP literature, it would have been nice to see an 
additional example rather than re-expressing
an existing one.

Not sure how useful the descriptions of the viewpoint 
concepts are, I suspect most people at the w/s will be 
familiar with them, or could read about them in existing 
literature.


- Strong points of the paper:

A carefully thought through UML profile, nicely illustrating 
that UML 2.0 is far more useable for this type of specification 
than previous versions.

- Any suggestions for improvements:

More detail on the example, less on the RM-ODP background.

5) FURTHER COMMENTS FOR AUTHORS:

Have you considered the use of a Domain Specific Language 
rather than a UML profile. Or at least compared the DSL 
approach with the profile approach.
