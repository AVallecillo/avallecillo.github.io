An interesting paper, but I am suspicious of the mish-mash 
of (probably incompatible) ideas that it attempts to synthesise. 
It is disappointing that the author has not consulted the key 
authority on the RM-ODP enterprise language - ISO 15414. 
Reference to Part 3 only has given her an inadequate understanding 
of the complexities and subtleties of the language.
 
Bearing this in mind, and the fact that most of the paper is not 
really about ODP at all, were it not for the fact that I'd do 
anything to promote awareness of ODP, I'd be inclined to 
reject the paper. However, she should probably be encouraged 
and so  I'd accept as a marginal.


- Summary and main contributions: 

Review of enterprise modelling approaches


- Weak points of the paper: 

Doesn't concentrate on ODP; makes no reference to 15414


- Strong points of the paper: 

At least someone is taking this seriously, we should encourage her

- Any suggestions for improvements:

She needs to look at ISO 15414 and bring that into the discussion
