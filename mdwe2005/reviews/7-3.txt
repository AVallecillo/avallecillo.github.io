4) JUSTIFICATION AND GENERAL COMMENTS:


The paper basically explains how to model portlets in a technology independent way and how to obtain platform specific models from the previous models. Unfortunately, the authors adopt a too wide approach and does not concentrate on the key aspects of the paper: how these models improve existing ones, say those supplied with OOHDM and WebML, and how these models can be translated in PSMs, that is, how technology impacts the transformation.


Currently, the paper is almost a bunch of standard UML models, without any real explanation of the advantages of such models, their differences with respect to other models, and the actual outcome (code) produced with such an approach.
