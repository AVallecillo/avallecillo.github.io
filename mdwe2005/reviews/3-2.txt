- Summary and main contributions:
The paper describes a PIM to a servlet-based PSM transformation for OOHDMDA which is OOHDM + business processes.  The paper focuses on the technical aspects of the transformation.

- Weak points of the paper:
The paper provides a lot of technical details that are not very interesting from an academic and scientific point of view. 
I am missing the challenges of this work. Everybody will believe that such a transformation is possible. 
The comparison with related work is rather brief and no justifications are given.

- Strong points of the paper:
A proof of concept that for the given situation a PIM to PSM transformation is possible

- Any suggestions for improvements:
More concentrate on the “Why’s”
