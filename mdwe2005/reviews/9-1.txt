4) JUSTIFICATION AND GENERAL COMMENTS:


Overall I found this paper to be well-written and with valuable
contributions to the literature in this area. The paper is structured
appropriately and relevant to the workshop. It could potentially have been
improved a little by providing a clearer consideration of related work in
the area - this tends to be a distributed throughout the paper making it a
little difficult to gain an overall view of how this work relates to other
similalr research. The paper could also benefit from a more structured
evaluation of how the proposed approach may actually be applied - possibly
through a structured case study? Probably the most problematic aspect of the
paper is that a number of the key aspects have only weak justifications
presented - though this is not a significant problem.
