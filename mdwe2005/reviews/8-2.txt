- Weak points of the paper:

The hard points of the approach are not really explained.

- Strong points of the paper:

The subject is interesting

- Any suggestions for improvements:
You should be more precise about the transformations applied in this paper.

The discourse is rather loose and too many generalities are provided.

Also use less acronyms (OO-H, UWE, etc.) and concentrate much more on 

the precise nature of your personal and original contribution

5) FURTHER COMMENTS FOR AUTHORS:

There are some wrong choices of words and some mispellings

even if the paper is easy to read.

The presentation of QVT is much too abstract.
Remember that a graphical presentation is far from capturing

the precise specification of a transformation.

The related section of your paper is very specific and selects only a few

of the published work. The rational for this specific selection of related

work remains unclear to the present reviewer.
