- Summary and main contributions:

The authors propose a framework to reduce the gap between business process modeling (BPR) and web systems design (WSD). The framework is presented as an extension of the UWA methodology (UWA+).

- Weak points of the paper:

I think the subject is important but you need to focus more on the contribution by expanding the description on how BPR can be integrated within the WSD.

The contribution of your paper starts in section 4. In a workshop-focused paper, perhaps it will be more interesting to expand this section and reduce sections 2 and 3. 

The running example is not at all clear. A set of questions arise to this reviewer. For example, in Figure 2 you present the piDiagram but what is the relation between this diagram and the standards diagrams provided by WDM such as navigation and/or presentation diagrams? In other words, how can a designer integrate in a navigation view the services belonging to the business model in a coherent way? If you describe this process in a detailed manner your paper would be improved and would be a subject of interest for the workshop audience. 
  

- Strong points of the paper:

The problem that is addressed in the paper is very interesting for the web engineering community. 

Well-written paper with an excellent background.


- Any suggestions for improvements:

See section "weak points". 

Also, you should try to describe the relationship of your work to the MDD/MDA initiative. This is one of the main goals of the workshop.
