- Summary and main contributions: 

This paper argues why it is necessary to 
revisit our web development methodologies so as to address 
directly the concept of business process, instead of
adopting a data-centric or system process-centric approach.

- Weak points of the paper:
  o The paper is not self-contained: it is almost required to read other
    references about the UWA framework to understand what are the extensions
    brought in the UWA+ framework. My recommendation here would be that a
    small section reminds us the major concepts of UWA (or a quick tour of it),
    then a subsequent section that lists, defines, and motivates all
    extensions brought to the initial framework.
  o The portion of the paper devoted to the motivations, although very well
    written, is perhaps too large with respect to the portion
    dedicated to the framework extension and the example: try to achieve a
    better balance.
  o The "travel agency" example in section 4.1 is rather disconnected from
    the section 4 dedicated to UWA+. My recommendation would be to make the
    example presented as a running example. For this purpose, structure
    section 4 into more subsections corresponding to major steps
    and provides the material that is produced as output for each step
    related to the example. Of course, this could be long. So, focus
    only on vital parts. In this way, it would be possible to follow the
    methodology while having the example at hand all the time.
  o Provide more material on the example


- Strong points of the paper:
  o There is an excellent discussion demonstrating why there is a need to
    consider business process as first-class citizens.
  o This discussion is very well motivated and positioned with respect to
    other families of methodologies, but not to precise methodologies (e.g.,
    WebML, OOHDM, etc.)
  o The example clearly shows the connexion between involved models and
    their future usage. This should be emphasized as this is
    the most original part of the paper.


- Any suggestions for improvements: 
  See above in th "weak points" part



5) FURTHER COMMENTS FOR AUTHORS:
   o provide a quick tour of UWA first
   o list, define and motivate the extensions brought to UWA+ afterward
   o present the example as a running one by structuring section 4 into
     steps and sub-steps.
