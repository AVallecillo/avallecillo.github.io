- Summary and main contributions:
The paper describes how Aspect-Oriented modeling and web services can be combined in an MDA approach. The authors illustrate Aspect-Oriented modeling for a number of concerns: logging, security and Travel-Agent Composition. Next it is discussed how the transformation from the PIM to the PSM can be done. Different alternatives are discussed

- Weak points of the paper:
Most of the material is discussed on the surface, e.g. the transformation alternatives are described but not elaborated, neither does the paper identifies possible problems but these can be a good issue for discussion at the workshop

- Strong points of the paper:
The approach presented is interesting and promising. 

- Any suggestions for improvements:
Add a small explanation about the principles of Aspect Oriented Modeling. 
Identify possible problem areas
