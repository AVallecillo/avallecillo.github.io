- Summary and main contributions:

The authors propose an extension of the W2000 Modeling Language to describe multilayer systems. The paper presents a case stuyd (The MicroLAB). Main contribution: The extension of W2000 notation to support one more level of aggregation in conceptual structures.
   
- Weak points of the paper:

No clear contributions. A simple presentation of an implementation of a case study (the MicroLAB). The paper does not fit well with the main topics of the conference. Paper too short. The web design does not appear in any section of the paper. The main contribution (the fractal approach) is at most 1/2 page long.

- Strong points of the paper:

None.

- Any suggestions for improvements: 
The paper should be rewritten and extended including information about how this solution/extension helps W2000 to Web Design. 
The contribution is not clear and the minimal extension to W2000 can not be considered one step beyond in the web engineering discipline.
