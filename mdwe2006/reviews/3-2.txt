- Summary and main contributions:

The paper describes the development process of the UWE defined as a model-driven development approach describing the metamodels and transformation involved in the process. 
   
- Weak points of the paper:

Sometimes there is an explosion of notations (metamodels) and it is not clear what is the contribution of the paper with respect to previous works (which are though correctly referred).

- Strong points of the paper:

I believe it is a neat, well written paper and structured paper. The content is presented with aboundance of examples and diagrams illustrating the approach. Despite the comments above I believe this work describe the overall development process with UWE.

- Any suggestions for improvements: 
I would make more clear what is the contribution of the paper - I believe just having a sistematic description of what has been done elsewhere is already relevant to the scope of the conference. Moreover, I would summarize the different metamodels involved and the kind of transformations used (with the kind of language, abstraction level, ect) in a two tables.
