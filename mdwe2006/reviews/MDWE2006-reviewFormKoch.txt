- Summary and main contributions:
The paper presents the development process of the UWE Web Engineering approach focusing on the model transformations aspects of the process. The main contribution of the paper is the global overview of the method that integrates several "external" techniques and models (WebRE and WebSA) but focusing on the transformation techniques (model-to-model or model-to-code) that have been applied or that are going to be applied in order to provide a complete MDD web engineering method. The main contribution could be the study and the application of several transformation techniques in the context of MDA and the integration of models and techniques in UWE.
   
- Weak points of the paper:

Due to space constraints I miss a more detailed presentation of how the techniques are applied and the drawbacks and problems that has been detected. It seems that it does not exist a CASE tool that integrates all the transformations that are presented in table 1. 

- Strong points of the paper:

Good overview of transformation techniques in the context of MDA and an interesting effort for the integration of models and techniques in a Model Driven fashion.


- Any suggestions for improvements: 

The paper should include some analysis or information about the adequateness of each transformation technique for each step in the MDD process.
