4) JUSTIFICATION AND GENERAL COMMENTS:


- Summary and main contributions:
   
The authors present an approach to verify the correctness of a web system by the usage of the formal modelling language SO-SAM. Therefore a translation from the component and connector view (an extension of UML) to SO-SAM is introduced. SO-SAM is based on Petri nets and on linear temporal logic used for the expression of temporal constraints. The validation is done by a model checker. 
The method proposed by the authors to validate a web system is very interesting but the content and contribution of this paper gets lost by numerous technical inaccuracies and the style of presentation. Because of this, many parts of the paper are hardly comprehensible. 


- Weak points of the paper:

	The semantics of the temporal logic is incompletely specified.
	Some linguistic problems make the paper hard to read.
	The font size in the pictures is very small.


- Strong points of the paper:

       an example scenario
       many pictures

- Any suggestions for improvements: 

The removal of the grammatical and linguistic errors would improve the readability enormously. Maybe you could ask a native English speaker to review the paper.

You should format the formulas in a different style (not italic for the names of variables in the formula text) and separate them from the text (especially on page 6).

What is the aim of the image processing scenario you use as running example through the whole paper? You describe how it is modelled but you should also spend a few words on what it actually does.
